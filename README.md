### **China Survival**

#### **游戏简介 / Game Overview**

《China Survival》是一场关于在中国境内进行短期旅行的极限模拟游戏。你将扮演一名外籍游客，面对陌生的数字环境、语言障碍与繁琐的行政流程。你的目标不仅是完成旅行，更要在有限的资金、精神值与时间限制下，生存并抵达旅程终点——香港。这是一场考验你如何应对“技术型焦虑”与“行政壁垒”的策略模拟。

*China Survival* is an extreme simulation game about short-term travel within China. You play as a foreign tourist navigating an unfamiliar digital environment, language barriers, and cumbersome administrative processes. Your goal is not only to complete your travels but to survive and reach your final destination, Hong Kong, under limited funds, sanity, and time. It is a strategic simulation that tests how you cope with "technical anxiety" and "administrative barriers."

---

#### **剧情推进机制：如何玩？ / Gameplay Progression**

游戏采用 **“状态驱动（State-Driven）”** 的分支剧情模式，剧情推进完全由你的资源决策决定：

The game utilizes a **"State-Driven"** branching narrative model, where progression is entirely determined by your resource decisions:

**1. 初始抉择 (Visa Selection)**

* **中文**：游戏起始于签证选择，直接决定你的初始时间压力。
* **English**：The game begins with a visa selection, which directly dictates your initial time pressure.

**2. 核心技术过关 (Tutorial Stages)**

* **中文**：进入北京后，你需要通过 SIM 卡办理、支付设置与景点预约三道关卡，这是进入后续游戏的必要门槛。
* **English**：Upon arriving in Beijing, you must clear three hurdles: SIM card setup, payment configuration, and attraction reservations. These are mandatory prerequisites for the rest of the game.

**3. 城市探索 (Map-based Exploration)**

* **中文**：完成验证后解锁中国地图。每个城市节点都是一个“事件集装箱”，点击城市触发如交通延误、文化冲击等随机任务。
* **English**：Unlock the map after verification. Each city node acts as an "event container," where clicking triggers random tasks such as transportation delays or culture shocks.

**4. 资源循环与生存压力 (Survival Loop)**

* **中文**：系统实时监控 `sanity`（精神值）与 `money`（余额）。资源分配决定了你的状态，例如精神值耗尽会导致某些剧情无法触发。
* **English**：The system monitors `sanity` and `money` in real-time. Resource allocation defines your status—for example, depleted sanity may block specific narrative triggers.

**5. 胜利条件 (Victory Condition)**

* **中文**：通过完成城市事件累积解锁点，规划前往香港的最终路径。
* **English**：Accumulate unlock points by completing city events to plot your final route to Hong Kong.
